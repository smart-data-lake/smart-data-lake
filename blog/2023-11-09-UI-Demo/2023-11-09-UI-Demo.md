We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new [UI tool](https://ui-demo.smartdatalake.ch/) designed to revolutionize the way visualize, track, and manage data flow! This extension of SDLB  acts as a user-friendly data catalog that showcases dependencies through a DAG, along with metadata for data objects and actions. Our UI tool is a step forward in data pipeline management, providing enhanced visibility and control. Whether you are a data engineer, analyst, or scientist, this tool will enhance your workflow management experience, allowing you to optimize processes and achieve exceptional results.

## 📊 Comprehensive Workflow Dashboard
Get a bird's eye view of your entire workflow! Our intuitive dashboard visualizes intricate details for each run, including execution time, duration, and the feed used. Dive deep into the timeline of  involved actions, with the ability to access detailed data by simply clicking on each action.
![Timeline of Action](timeline.png "Timeline of Action: Description or Caption")


## 🔍 Config Viewer with Rich Content
Explore an expansive Config Viewer that contains all the information from your config file. And extra information you provide as documentation. You can find:

* ###  Markdown Descriptions:
  Our tool supports markdown files as descriptions, enabling you to add detailed information about actions or objects. These descriptions can include images, hyperlinks, and other formatting options, serve as excellent documentation, aiding your understanding of the workflow's structure and function. Moreover, you can utilize descriptions to add labels by incorporating metadata in the config file, enhancing organization and clarity.
  ![descirption](btl-distance-des.png "Description of dataobject btl-disctance")
  ![metadata-label](config-int_airports.png "Config view of int-airports with ID in Green and Labels from meatadata in brown ")
  

* ### Clear Visualization of Transformations:
  For actions involving transformations, our tool provides clear visualizations within the configuration. Easily comprehend input and output data for each action, with seamless links to corresponding data objects. This feature simplifies the understanding of complex data transformations, ensuring transparency and efficiency in your workflow.
![transformer](transformer.png "Action View of historize-airports")

* ### Data Lineage Graph:
  Track and understand the flow of data objects across various processing stages with our built-in lineage feature. Visualize the relationships between data objects and actions involved in your data pipeline, empowering you to monitor the data's journey and make informed decisions.
  ![lineage](config-int_airports_lineage.png "Lineage of dataobject int-airports with invloved action id")
