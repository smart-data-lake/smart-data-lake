We are thrilled to announce the launch of our new [UI tool](https://ui-demo.smartdatalake.ch/) designed to revolutionize the way we visualize, track, and manage data pipelines! This addition to SDLB acts as a user-friendly technical data catalog that showcases dependencies through a graph, along with metadata about data pipeline execution (workflows). Our UI tool is a step forward in data pipeline management, providing enhanced visibility and control. Whether you are a data engineer, analyst, or scientist, this tool will enhance your abilities to management data pipelines, allowing you to be more productive and achieve exceptional results.

## 📊 Comprehensive Workflow Dashboard
Get a bird's eye view of your entire workflow! Our intuitive dashboard visualizes intricate details for each run, including execution time, duration, and the feed configuration used. Deep dive into the timeline of involved actions, with the ability to access detailed data by simply clicking on each action.
![Timeline of Action](timeline.png "Timeline of Action: Description or Caption")


## 🔍 Config Viewer with Rich Content
Explore an expansive Configuration Viewer that contains all the information from your config file, and extra information you provide as documentation. You can find:


* ###  Markdown Descriptions:
  SDLB supports creating descriptions as markdown files, enabling you to provide detailed information about actions or objects. These descriptions can include images, hyperlinks, and other formatting options. They serve as excellent documentation that is versionned together with your code, aiding your understanding of the workflow's structure and function. Moreover, you can utilize tags to enhance organization and clarity.

  ![description](btl-distance-des.png "Description of dataobject btl-distance")

  ![metadata-label](config-int_airports.png "Config view of int-airports with ID in Green and Labels from metadata in brown ")

  

* ### Clear Visualization of Transformations:
  For actions involving transformations, our tool provides clear visualizations within the configuration. Easily comprehend input and output data for each action, with seamless links to corresponding data objects. This feature simplifies the understanding of complex data transformations, ensuring transparency about the business logic applied.

![transformer](transformer.png "Action View of historize-airports")

* ### Data Lineage Graph:
  Track and understand the flow of data across various processing stages with our built-in lineage feature. Visualize the dependencies between data objects and actions involved in your data pipeline, empowering you to monitor the data's journey and answer questions about the impact of changes.

  ![lineage](config-int_airports_lineage.png "Lineage of dataobject int-airports with involved action id")
